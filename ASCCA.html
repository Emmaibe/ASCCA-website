WHO WE ARE

All Saints' Chapel Choir Alumni, is the Alumni body of the All Saints' Chapel Choir, University of Benin, Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria. We are more than just a group, we are a family of classical music singers, who passed through our parent choir together.

ABOUT ALL SAINTS' CHAPEL CHOIR, UNIBEN

ASCC is the choir unit of the All Saint's Chapel, University of Benin in Edo State, Nigeria. We are dedicated to bringing great worship to the members of the church through Hymns, Chants, Classical pieces and other great christian songs.

ASCC choristers comprises of students, lecturers, staffs of the University and members of the church from other forms of occupation. We are truly a diverse mix that represents Nigerian's culture.

Our Vision

ASCC acts as a catalyst for encouring and nuturing students to be closer to God through Music.

Our Mission

ASCC is a group of musicians dedicated to giving hope and drawing people closer to God through the excellent performance of quality christain music.

Our Commitment

  • Sing Hymns, Chants, Classical pieces and other great christian songs during Service to lift the spirit of members
  • Engage great people to perform great music.
  • Present concerts that are a place of respite and encouragement.
  • Help emerging and established conductors and pianists refine their skills.
